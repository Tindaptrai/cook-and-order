THIET KE BACKEND FOODIELAB - DACS_FOOD

1. Phan tich nghiep vu

Website FoodieLab la website ban do an ket hop cong thuc nau an. Backend can quan ly cac luong chinh:
- Khach hang xem menu, loc theo danh muc, xem chi tiet mon an.
- Khach hang them mon vao gio hang, tao don hang, chon COD hoac QR chuyen khoan demo.
- Khach hang dang ky, dang nhap, xac thuc OTP Gmail.
- He thong chong spam gui OTP theo email va IP.
- He thong quan ly 10 ban: 2 ban private, 8 ban thuong.
- He thong ap ma giam gia gio vang trong 2 khung 10:30-12:30 va 16:00-18:00.
- Thanh vien than thiet duoc dung ma giam gia voi tan suat 1 tuan/lien.
- Admin quan ly mon an, danh muc, don hang, nguoi dung, ban va ma giam gia.

2. Database schema de xuat

Nguoi dung:
- ApplicationUser ke thua IdentityUser: FullName, IsEmailVerified, LoyaltyLevel, CreatedAt.

OTP:
- EmailOtps: Id, UserId, Email, CodeHash, Purpose, ExpiresAt, UsedAt, FailedAttempts, CreatedAt.
- OtpSendLogs: Id, Email, IpAddress, Purpose, SentAt.

Mon an:
- FoodCategories: Id, Name, Slug, Description, IsActive, SortOrder.
- FoodItems: Id, FoodCategoryId, Name, Slug, Description, Ingredients, Story, Price, ImageUrl, Tag, IsBestSeller, IsActive, CreatedAt, UpdatedAt.

Cong thuc:
- Recipes: Id, FoodItemId, Title, Slug, Description, Ingredients, Steps, ImageUrl, Difficulty, CookingTimeMinutes, IsActive, CreatedAt.

Gio hang:
- Carts: Id, UserId, SessionId, CreatedAt, UpdatedAt.
- CartItems: Id, CartId, FoodItemId, Quantity, UnitPrice.

Don hang:
- Orders: Id, OrderCode, UserId, CustomerName, PhoneNumber, Address, OrderType, TableId, Subtotal, DiscountAmount, TotalAmount, PaymentMethod, PaymentStatus, OrderStatus, DiscountCodeId, CreatedAt.
- OrderItems: Id, OrderId, FoodItemId, FoodName, UnitPrice, Quantity, LineTotal.

Thanh toan:
- Payments: Id, OrderId, Method, Amount, QrContent, BankAccountName, BankAccountNumber, BankName, Status, CreatedAt, PaidAt.

Ban:
- RestaurantTables: Id, TableNumber, Name, TableType, Capacity, Status, IsActive.

Ma giam gia:
- DiscountCodes: Id, Code, Name, DiscountType, DiscountValue, MinOrderAmount, MaxDiscountAmount, UsageLimit, UsedCount, DiscountScope, StartAt, EndAt, IsActive.
- DiscountUsages: Id, DiscountCodeId, UserId, OrderId, UsedAt.

3. Models, Controllers, Services can tao

Models:
- ApplicationUser, FoodCategory, FoodItem, Recipe, Cart, CartItem, Order, OrderItem, Payment, RestaurantTable, DiscountCode, DiscountUsage, EmailOtp, OtpSendLog.
- Enums: PaymentMethod, PaymentStatus, OrderStatus, OrderType, TableType, TableStatus, DiscountType, DiscountScope, OtpPurpose.
- ViewModels: MenuViewModel, FoodDetailViewModel, CartViewModel, CheckoutViewModel, CreateOrderViewModel, LoginViewModel, RegisterViewModel, VerifyOtpViewModel.

Controllers:
- HomeController: trang chu va cac trang noi dung co san.
- MenuController: menu, loc danh muc, chi tiet mon.
- CartController: xem gio, them, cap nhat, xoa.
- OrderController: checkout, tao don, xem thanh cong.
- PaymentController: QR demo, xac nhan thanh toan demo.
- AccountController: dang ky, dang nhap, xac thuc OTP, gui lai OTP, dang xuat.
- TableController: danh sach ban.
- Admin area: DashboardController, FoodItemsController, OrdersController, UsersController, TablesController, DiscountCodesController.

Services:
- IFoodService, ICategoryService, ICartService, IOrderService, IPaymentService, IEmailSender, IOtpService, IOtpRateLimitService, ITableService, IDiscountService.

Buoc 1: Them EF Core, Identity va ApplicationDbContext.
Buoc 2: Tao entity va enum.
Buoc 3: Seed danh muc, mot so mon mau, 10 ban va ma giam gia.
Buoc 4: Tao service menu/category/table/discount/order/cart/payment/otp.
Buoc 5: Tao controller public.
Buoc 6: Tao admin area nen.
Buoc 7: Chuyen tung man hinh frontend tu JS tam sang du lieu database.
Buoc 8: Hoan thien OTP Gmail va chong spam.
Buoc 9: Kiem thu luong dat mon, thanh toan COD/QR, ap ma giam gia, admin cap nhat don.
