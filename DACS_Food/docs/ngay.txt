NHAT KY CONG VIEC HOM NAY - DACS_FOOD / FOODIELAB

1. Kiem tra va tong quan project
- Doc cau truc project ASP.NET Core MVC DACS_Food.
- Xac nhan frontend FoodieLab da duoc dua vao cau truc MVC voi Views, Layout va wwwroot/assets.
- Kiem tra build ban dau bang dotnet build.

2. Thiet ke nghiep vu backend
- Phan tich nghiep vu website ban do an va cong thuc nau an.
- De xuat database schema cho mon an, danh muc, gio hang, don hang, thanh toan, OTP, ban, ma giam gia va admin.
- De xuat Models, Controllers, Services, Routes va thu tu trien khai.
- Tao file BACKEND_NGHIEP_VU_FOODIELAB.txt de luu lai thiet ke nghiep vu.

3. Xay dung backend nen
- Them EF Core SQL Server va ASP.NET Core Identity.
- Tao ApplicationDbContext.
- Tao ApplicationUser va cac entity nghiep vu:
  FoodCategory, FoodItem, Recipe, Cart, CartItem, Order, OrderItem, Payment, RestaurantTable, DiscountCode, DiscountUsage, EmailOtp, OtpSendLog.
- Tao enum nghiep vu:
  PaymentMethod, PaymentStatus, OrderStatus, OrderType, TableType, TableStatus, DiscountType, DiscountScope, OtpPurpose.
- Seed du lieu danh muc mon, mot so mon mau, 10 ban, ma giam gia gio vang va ma thanh vien than thiet.
- Tao DatabaseSeeder de khoi tao database, role Admin/Customer va tai khoan admin mac dinh.

4. Tai khoan va xac thuc
- Them dang ky, dang nhap, dang xuat bang Identity.
- Them OTP Gmail ve mat nghiep vu:
  Tao OTP, hash OTP, het han OTP, xac thuc OTP.
- Them chong spam OTP:
  Gioi han gui lai trong 60 giay, gioi han theo gio, theo ngay va theo IP.
- Them validation cho form dang nhap/dang ky de bam trong khong bi loi.
- Them menu tai khoan sau khi dang nhap:
  Ten tai khoan, hang thanh vien, thong tin tai khoan, lich su don hang, dang xuat.
- Tao trang /tai-khoan va /lich-su-don-hang.

5. Admin
- Tao Admin Area nen.
- Them route /admin.
- Them cac trang admin nen:
  Mon an, don hang, nguoi dung, ban, ma giam gia.
- Tao file admin.txt chua tai khoan admin mac dinh:
  admin@foodielab.local / Admin@123.

6. Swagger API
- Cai Swashbuckle.AspNetCore.
- Bat Swagger UI tai /swagger.
- Them API test:
  GET /api/foods
  GET /api/foods/{id}
  GET /api/foods/slug/{slug}
  GET /api/foods/best-sellers
  GET /api/tables
  GET /api/tables/{id}/slots
  PUT /api/tables/{id}/status
  GET /api/discounts/golden-hour
  GET /api/discounts/calculate
  GET /api/orders/{orderCode}

7. Menu va mon an
- Tao MenuController va route /menu, /menu/{slug}.
- Chuyen nut menu trong layout sang route /menu.
- Them trang menu backend lay du lieu tu database.
- Them trang chi tiet mon.
- Giu popup chi tiet mon an va chatbot noi tren layout.

8. Gio hang va thanh toan
- Nang cap gio hang frontend:
  Khong hien so 0 khi gio hang trong.
  Khi them mon moi tang so luong tren nut Gio hang.
  Popup gio hang hien hinh anh mon, ten mon, don gia, so luong, thanh tien.
  Them nut tang, giam, xoa tung mon.
- Checkout nhan du lieu gio hang tu localStorage khi server cart chua co.
- Neu da dang nhap, checkout tu dien ten nguoi dung.
- Nguoi dung nhap so dien thoai, dia chi va chon phuong thuc thanh toan.
- Tao OrderService de tao don hang, order items, tinh tong tien.
- Tao PaymentService cho COD va QR demo.
- Sua nghiep vu:
  COD khong hien QR demo.
  QR moi hien trang QR.
- Sau khi dat hang thanh cong thi clear gio hang.
- Trang dat hang thanh cong co thong bao than thien:
  Nha hang da nhan don, nhan vien se goi lai xac nhan ten, so dien thoai, dia chi.
  Huong dan khach lien he hotline/Facebook/Zalo neu sai thong tin.

9. Ma giam gia
- Them DiscountService.
- Ho tro ma gio vang GIOVANG10.
- Ho tro ma thanh vien than thiet THANTHIET15.
- Kiem tra gio vang 10:30-12:30 va 16:00-18:00.
- Gioi han ma thanh vien than thiet 1 tuan/lien.

10. Quan ly ban va dat ban
- Doi ten menu "Quan ly ban" thanh "Dat ban & Khong gian".
- Them section tinh trang ban tren trang chu.
- Chuyen section tinh trang ban xuong duoi Best seller.
- Xoa section "Website co gi?" tren trang chu.
- Tao nghiep vu dat ban rieng:
  TableReservation, ReservationStatus.
- Trang public /ban chi hien thong tin cong khai:
  So ban, loai ban Private/Thuong, suc chua, trang thai Con trong/Dang ban.
- Khong hien ten khach, so dien thoai, email, ghi chu noi bo cho nguoi dung.
- Nguoi dung co form gui yeu cau dat ban:
  Ngay dat, ban, gio dat, ho ten, so dien thoai, ghi chu.
- Sau khi khach gui yeu cau, chi hien thong bao:
  FoodieLab da nhan thong tin dat ban va nhan vien se lien he lai de xac nhan.
- Admin co form dat ban nang cao:
  Chon ngay, chon ban, chon khung gio, ten khach, so dien thoai, ghi chu, trang thai Pending/Confirmed.
- Khi admin chon ngay va ban, he thong tai danh sach khung gio theo interval 30 phut.
- Moi luot dat ban mac dinh giu 90 phut.
- Neu dat 12:00 thi 12:00, 12:30, 13:00 bi khoa cho cung ban.
- Pending va Confirmed se khoa khung gio.
- Completed va Cancelled khong khoa khung gio nua.
- Admin co the chuyen trang thai dat ban sang Completed hoac Cancelled de mo lai khung gio.
- Quan mo cua 08:00-22:00.
- Chi nhan dat ban tu 08:30 den 21:00 de tranh sat gio mo/dong cua.
- Da xoa cau "quan co the ap dung phu thu phuc vu" trong cac View.
